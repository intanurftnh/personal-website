My First Impression Of Joining
The Jobready Web Development Bootcamp

I was able to join this jobready web development bootcamp because I got a scholarship for winning the Samsung Innovation Campus bootcamp last year. This bootcamp started when I just graduated from vocational school. This bootcamp starts from August to December 2023. Right now my daily life is only focused on this bootcamp.

This bootcamp begins with doing individual HTML, CSS, and Javacript material exercises. Then the participants including me were grouped into 5 groups of 3 people each. Once I found new groups and friends, we were given the task of creating digital ideas and solutions. Then create an app that can be used by everyone

My first impression of attending this bootcamp was to feel nervous and scared because I met other friends who might be better at programming. But I am happy to have the opportunity to make new friends who come from out of town and also have the opportunity to get guidance from a very great new mentor.

I am currently in the learning process and starting to find difficulties because I am learning higher than the previous bootcamp. But I will try to learn better so that I can gain more knowledge than before.
